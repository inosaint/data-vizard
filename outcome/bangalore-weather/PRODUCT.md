# Product Context: Bangalore Weather Rhythms

## Audience

This artifact is primarily for local Data Vizard workflow testing, but it should still read like a public-facing piece for people interested in Bangalore climate, long-term weather patterns, and seasonal rhythms.

## Purpose

Turn long-run hourly weather records for Bangalore into a visualization that makes the city’s rain, heat, and wind structure legible without collapsing into a generic weather dashboard.

## Personality

Calm, observant, and data-led. More climate field guide than app widget.

## Anti-References

- Do not make this look like a forecast app.
- Do not make this look like a generic line-chart dashboard with filters.
- Do not imply certainty beyond the observed historical records.

## Design Principles

- Let the weather pattern lead before the explanatory text.
- Respect seasonality and long time span.
- Keep the artifact focused on one strong reading path at a time.
- Preserve the difference between mean conditions and extreme events.

## Accessibility Goals

- Strong contrast in all primary states.
- Keyboard-reachable interaction if marks are interactive.
- Reduced-motion safe behavior.
- Exact values available without relying on color alone.
