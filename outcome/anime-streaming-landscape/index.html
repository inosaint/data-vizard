The Anime Orbit

Posters drift by reach, era, and genre. Pick one to pull it into focus.

Source: `anime_dataset.csv`.

About this view

How to read it Titles with broader platform reach and stronger ratings drift closer to the center. The alternate `Era drift` layout re-groups the same catalog by decade.
What this is good for Discovery, clustering, and browsing one title at a time.
Important caveats This is a curated catalog snapshot, not a full streaming census. `rating` and `popularity` are heavily bucketed and should be read as rough tiers, not precise rank claims. Platform presence reflects this file only, not full catalog share or regional licensing.
