Sources and method

How to read: select a month, or press Play, to move month by month and change the bird list. The bars use GBIF occurrence evidence, mostly eBird human observations, for 10 selected wetland, migrant, winter-visitor, or seasonal species in a Bengaluru-area query from 2014-2026. Each bar pools one calendar month across those years; January is the reference month.

The bird list ranks species by how close the selected month is to each species' own monthly high. Photos and Wikipedia links are loaded from the project-local Wikipedia/Wikimedia metadata file.
