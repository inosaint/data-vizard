Pick The Shape Of The Sky

Same story, three constellations. Each preview uses the real availability distribution in the dataset: 53 titles on one platform, 101 on two, 45 on three, and 7 on four.

A

Deep Field

A free-floating night sky where dense low-availability titles form a glowing cloud and the rare wide-availability titles flare brighter and farther out.

D3/SVG Studio Hover tooltips
Best for mood and data art. The first read is emotional, then exploratory.
B

Orbit Rings

Titles sit on four orbital bands by platform count, turning the dataset into a celestial system with a clearer structure and easier legend-free reading.

D3/SVG Teaching Hover tooltips
Best for clarity. Slightly less poetic, but very easy to read.
C

Platform Web

A star map anchored by platform hubs, with titles bridging services like drawn constellations across the sky.

D3/SVG Editorial Hover tooltips
Best if you want the platforms themselves to feel like named constellations.

All three assume a dark-sky palette, `Space Grotesk`, minimal text, and hover metadata for title, year, rating, genres, and platform presence.
