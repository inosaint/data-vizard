# Narrator Story Brief: India Literacy Choropleth

## Frame

Explanatory public-interest map.

## Audience

General readers who need to understand the metric without data-analysis background.

## Main Question

Where do literacy rates stand across India?

## Opening Copy

Title: Where literacy rates stand across India

Subtitle: The NSO 2017-18 education survey estimated literacy among people aged seven and older. The map shows how that measure varies across states and union territories.

## Tooltip Language

- Region name
- Literacy rate: `x.x%`

## Empty State

Select a region.

Choose any state or union territory to see its estimated literacy rate.

## Caveat Language

Share of people aged seven and older reported as literate in the NSO 2017-18 household social consumption survey.

## Handoff To Designer

Keep the map as the primary visual. Avoid visible implementation notes in the hero. Put map and data source credits in the footer.
