# FIFA 2026 World Cup group-stage visualization context

## Current brief

Explore what can be created from a Kaggle-style match dataset for the 2026 FIFA World Cup group stage.

## Current scope

- Use group-stage matches only.
- Exclude the single Round of 32 row from the original source file.
- Treat the analysis as descriptive, not predictive.

## Audience and format

Audience, narrative angle, visual style, and final artifact format are still open decisions.

## Accessibility goals

- Keep claims visible with caveats.
- Use readable labels and color choices that work for color-vision deficiencies.
- Avoid relying on color alone for team or outcome differences.
