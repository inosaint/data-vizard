# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

General public readers who should understand the story without needing a data-analysis background.

## Product Purpose

Build a public-facing narrative showing how snow changed NYC yellow taxi pickup patterns, moving from citywide demand to area-level disruption.

## Brand Personality

Civic, spatial, direct, and cinematic. The experience should feel like a map-led public-interest data story, not a dashboard or a chart notebook.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic AI dashboards, awkward default bar charts, consulting slide-deck layouts, Mapbox demo clones, and overproduced news graphics that bury the point in decoration.

## Design Principles

- Lead with the map when geography is the story.
- Move from citywide context to a precise area spotlight.
- Make the numbers visible without making the page feel like an analytics tool.
- Use motion to guide attention, not to decorate.
- Keep caveats close to the claims they qualify.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA. Maintain readable contrast, avoid color-only meaning, support reduced motion, and keep the story understandable without map manipulation.
