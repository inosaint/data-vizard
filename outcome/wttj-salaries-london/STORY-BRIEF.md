# Story Brief

## Selected Direction

Fastest climbers

## Posture

Explanatory

## Language Load

Guided narrative

## Text Budget

Moderate. Each scroll step should earn one main takeaway and one supporting comparison.

## Must Keep Text

- The dataset covers Welcome to the Jungle salary trajectories for London roles.
- Salary growth is not linear across roles.
- Some roles with modest starting salaries become steep climbers by mid-career or senior level.

## Optional Text

- Caveat that not all roles have full year-0 coverage.
- Short note that the dataset reflects modeled/aggregated salary trajectory data rather than single-company ladders.

## Focal Point

Which roles climb fastest from entry to senior pay, and when that climb becomes visible.

## Default To Avoid

Simple ranked bar charts that flatten the whole story into one “top 10” list.

## Scroll Logic

1. Establish the common baseline: many roles cluster around similar early-career medians.
2. Reveal divergence by years 3 to 5, where some roles pull away sharply.
3. Show the steepest climbers by slope and total gain.
4. Contrast fast climbers with strong starters that grow more steadily.
5. End on the idea that career salary trajectories depend on both starting point and acceleration.
